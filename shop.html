Rare Plants & Cuttings

Living Collection

We maintain over 300 species and cultivars of plants with documented provenance, including Philodendron, Monstera, Begonia, Selaginella, and miniature orchids. Many are threatened or vulnerable species not available anywhere else. All plants are propagated in cultivation or wild collected with permits for ex situ conservation. We collect ethically, with provenance, for the purpose of preserving threatened species.

Interested in available cuttings and rooted plants? Looking for rare and vulnerable species for your own Ark?

Send us an email at TheVioletRoseAtelier@gmail.com with your wishlist. We will let you know what we have propagating and help you source what you are looking for.
